CTF Night 0x1: Beginner-Friendly CTF

The Cyber Security Club, Uttara University is excited to announce CTF Night 0x1 - our first beginner-friendly Capture The Flag competition! This event is designed specifically for students who are new to cybersecurity and want to learn while having fun.

What is CTF Night 0x1?

CTF Night 0x1 is a beginner-focused cybersecurity competition where participants solve puzzles and challenges to find hidden "flags." It's the perfect entry point for students interested in exploring the exciting world of cybersecurity!

Event Details

  • Date: Saturday, August 10, 2025
  • Time: 8:00 PM - 1:00 AM
  • Venue: ctf-cybersecurity-club-uttarauniversity.ac.bd
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Format: Solo

Challenge Categories

Our beginner-friendly challenges will cover essential cybersecurity fundamentals:

  • Web Security (Easy)
  • Cryptography (Beginner)
  • Digital Forensics (Intro)
  • General Security (Fundamentals)

Prizes and Recognition

Top performers will receive Club Exclusive swag items!

Registration Requirements

  • Must be a student at Uttara University
  • Must be registered for the event
  • Open to all skill levels, especially beginners
  • Teams of 1 (solo participation)

Contact Information

Questions? Contact our CTF Committee:

  • Email: ctf@cybersecurity.club.uttara.ac.bd
  • Discord: Join our Discord
  • Facebook: CSC Uttara University

Ready to Join?

Registration Link: cybersecurity.club.uttara.ac.bd/ctf-registration

Registration Deadline: August 10, 2025

Don't miss this fantastic opportunity to start your cybersecurity journey! Whether you're a complete beginner or have some basic knowledge, CTF Night 0x1 is designed to be educational, challenging, and most importantly - fun!

See you at CTF Night 0x1!
